Understanding Self-Tracking Practices in Enigmatic Disease Management

An UX Research Project


The Problem

Self-tracking tools are often built around the assumption that tracking the “right” health variables will lead to actionable insights and greater control over one’s health. Yet, it remains unclear how these assumptions hold up in contexts marked by uncertainty, unpredictability, and frequent fluctuations in health needs.
